Ultimate Goal

Ultimate Goal, a First Tech Challenge (FTC) game. In this game you are required to move a set of Draidles, and Taurus's around the field into final goals. This season we only had about 2 weeks to build our robot, but we managed to pull though with an Inventive award

Our Final Robot

This is Rush, he was the robot for 2020-2021. His main goal was to pickup these taurus figures and move them into the goals, he also had to push around these wobble goals, which we nicknamed the Draidles. He did so very well winning an Inventive Award
